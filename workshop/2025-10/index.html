AI Agents for Work

Columbia University | October 27, 2025

How can AI Agents
streamline work and operations?

This workshop brings together industry leaders with academic experts at Columbia University to share the latest innovations and challenges for how AI agents can streamline work and operations.

The first day will be a series of public keynotes, talks and panel discussions from industry and academic experts. The second day will be a private workshop for partners affiliated with the Data, Agents and Processes Lab (DAPLab).

See the previous workshop here.

When: October 27, 2025

Where: Columbia Business School, Geffen Hall, 3rd Floor Directions

Time: 9:00-6pm, includes breakfast, lunch and a social hour with drinks
