Reliable and Trustworthy Agent Automation

DAPLab develops the infrastructure needed to make AI agents safe, reliable, and deployable in real-world organizations. Our work spans four tightly connected research thrusts:

Trustworthy Agent Execution
Sandboxing, branching, checkpointing, isolation, and stateful execution environments for agents.
Agent Safety and Governance
Data flow control, policy enforcement, provenance tracking, and trustworthy automation constraints.
Reliable Agent Evaluation
Benchmarks, exploratory QA, simulation environments, and continuous evaluation infrastructure.
Human-Agent Interaction and Usability
Interaction design, human oversight interfaces, collaborative workflows, and usability research for humans working with and supervising AI agents.
